Terminal options (powered by Global Payments)

Global Payments provides a full range of certified card payment terminals. Specific models are proposed during onboarding based on your business needs, environment, and approval outcome.

Smart POS (Android)

Touchscreen Smart POS terminals suitable for counter service, table service, and mobile use. Supports modern payment experiences and future extensibility.

Mobile terminals (SIM / Wi-Fi)

Portable terminals designed for merchants who need flexibility — cafés, delivery teams, pop-ups, events, and service professionals.

Countertop terminals (LAN / Wi-Fi)

Stable countertop devices for fixed cashier locations where reliability and speed are the priority.

Terminal model availability, features, and pricing are subject to Global Payments approval and acquiring bank requirements.

FAQ

What do you need from me to propose MDR?

Business type, estimated monthly card sales volume, number of outlets, and your current MDR (if available).

How fast can we go live?

Depends on document completeness and approval flow. Once approved, setup and training can be arranged quickly.

Can you support multi-outlet onboarding?

Yes — deployment checklist, outlet-by-outlet tracking guidance, and training materials.

Do you support POS integration?

We can discuss your POS environment and options during onboarding. Integrations may involve third-party work or fees depending on your POS vendor.
